Career Tracks

Explore the three core disciplines of quantitative finance and how we prepare you for them.

Quantitative Developer

Quant Devs build the lightning-fast infrastructure that executes trades in microseconds. They write low-latency C++ code, design massive data pipelines, and optimize systems to handle terabytes of market data.

Key Skills:

  • C++ / Systems Programming
  • Low-Latency Design
  • Distributed Systems (Kafka, Spark)
  • Python (Data Engineering)

Quantitative Trader

Quant Traders manage the execution of strategies, monitor risk in real-time, and identify short-term market inefficiencies. They rely on probability, game theory, and quick mental math to outmaneuver the market.

Key Skills:

  • Mental Math / Probability
  • Game Theory / Strategy
  • Market Microstructure
  • Risk Management
